Vegan Dinner Recipe: Vegan Stir-Fry

A quick and easy stir-fry with tons of fresh vegetables, tofu, and savory soy sauce. Perfect for a light and filling dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (optional)
  • Cooked rice to serve

Instructions:

  1. In a pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and add the tofu cubes. Cook until golden brown and crispy.
  2. Add the sliced bell pepper, broccoli, and carrot to the pan, and stir-fry until vegetables are tender.
  3. Drizzle with soy sauce and sesame oil, and toss everything to combine.
  4. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice and garnish with sesame seeds if desired.
  5. Enjoy your vegan stir-fry dinner!
