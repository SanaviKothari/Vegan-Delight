Vegan Breakfast Recipe: Avocado Toast

This simple and nutritious avocado toast recipe is a perfect way to start your day. Packed with healthy fats and vitamins, it's both filling and delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: red pepper flakes, cherry tomatoes, or sprouts for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.
  2. While the bread is toasting, mash the avocado with lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado on the toasted bread slices.
  4. Top with your desired garnishes like red pepper flakes or cherry tomatoes.
  5. Enjoy your healthy and delicious vegan breakfast!
