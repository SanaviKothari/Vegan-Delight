Vegan Lunch Recipe: Chickpea Salad

This chickpea salad is a quick and refreshing lunch option. It's loaded with protein, fiber, and fresh vegetablesâ€”perfect for a healthy mid-day meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: fresh parsley or cilantro

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the chickpeas, cucumber, onion, and bell pepper.
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Mix until everything is well combined.
  4. Optional: Garnish with parsley or cilantro.
  5. Serve chilled and enjoy!
